FAQS

What’s a “simple website” exactly?
It will be more detailed than a wireframe and much better looking than a common "Linktree" page. The final product will be a Figma website mockup, entirely made from scratch by humans. You will be able to highlight your brand's core offer and clearly stand out from AI-generated or template-based landing pages. Just remember, it will be a design mockup, a concept meant to be shared with developers or agencies; it won't be a fully-developed website.
How long will it take?
We try to deliver websites in under a week, but it depends on how many requests we get. The sooner you ask, the sooner you'll get your site, because we handle requests as they come in.
How many revisions are there included with the service?
We don't include major revisions, but we can make small tweaks. This service is designed to give you a head start, not a final polished product. It’s better than AI since it’s done by humans, but it is not as good as a $1,000 website.
How do I submit my first request?
The steps are outlined above, but basically: pay, write the text for your site along with a basic idea of how you want it to look, email it to us, and then just wait for your results.
Is this a prank? What’s the catch?
Sadly, this isn’t a prank, and happily, there’s no catch. This is the best way to make your wireframes today and upsell them at much higher costs for your current clients or brands.
Do you offer web development services as well?
Yes! If you loved the design, just ask our team for development via email, and we'll quickly chat about how to take this website concept into a live site, fast.
Who is this not for?
If you need a multi-page, complex e-commerce website, then this service might not be for you. If you are familiar with services like “Linktree,” this will be a step up, and you’ll only have to pay once. On the other hand, we also offer a more comprehensive design and development service at Design Stoic where we are open to working on larger projects overall.
Who are we? What do we stand for?
We are a web design agency based in Paraguay. It might sound like we live in the middle of nowhere, and in a way, we do. As a company, we aim to create a product that gives as much value as possible to people upfront to build a name for ourselves online, even without a large following (yet). Our main brand is called Design Stoic. There, we offer Webflow development and graphic design services, which at the moment is an offer unknown to mankind. This project is our best attempt to create a unique lead magnet. To everyone who chooses to work with us, we promise to give it our all to create a stunning website concept that will make your brand or store stand out online and drive better results.
What if the website mockup sucks?
We don’t usually offer refunds, but if the site really misses the mark and you’re not satisfied with the final result, we’re open to discussing a solution.
